## 1. Introduction

**GT-NEWSTECH** is committed to protecting your privacy. This page explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## 2. Data We Collect

### a. Data We Do Not Collect Directly
- No account registration or login is required.
- We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal data directly.

### b. Automatically Collected Data
- **Dark/Light mode preference:** Saved in `localStorage` on your device only — never sent to any server.
- **Visit analytics:** We may use anonymous analytics tools in the future (will be announced).

## Wokwi: Making Chip Design Accessible to Everyone

Have you ever dreamed of designing your own electronic chip?  
**Wokwi** makes you forget the limits.

Wokwi is the **world's most advanced ESP32 and microcontroller simulator**, running entirely inside your browser, with no software installation or real hardware needed during development.

🔗 **Official Website:** [wokwi.com](https://wokwi.com)

---

## What Does Wokwi Offer?

### 🖥️ Instant Simulation in the Browser
You can write code (Arduino, MicroPython, or Rust) and simulate your electronic circuit directly in the browser. Wokwi supports:
- **ESP32** all variants (including ESP32-S3, C3, and H2)
- **Arduino Uno, Mega, Leonardo**
- **Raspberry Pi Pico (RP2040)**
- **Common electronic components** (LCDs, buttons, sensors, LEDs, motors, GPS modules, etc.)

### 🤝 Integration with Tiny Tapeout
Thanks to the collaboration with **Tiny Tapeout**, you can turn your simulated design into a **real silicon chip**, custom-manufactured and shipped to your doorstep.

> What does this mean?  
> Anyone – hobbyist, student, engineer – can design a complete digital circuit, test it in the browser, then order a physical version at a very low cost.

## How to Get Started Now

1. Open [wokwi.com](https://wokwi.com)
2. Choose your microcontroller (ESP32, Arduino, RPi Pico)
3. Write code in the built-in editor
4. Add electronic components by clicking "+"
5. Run the simulation and see results instantly

To order a real chip:
- Design a digital circuit using Wokwi
- Go to the Tiny Tapeout section within the site
- Follow the instructions to convert your design to GDS files
- Pay the fabrication fee (varies by area)
- Receive your chip within weeks

## ARM Reshaping the PC Landscape

According to the latest **IDC** 2026 report, ARM chips now represent **35%** of all global laptop sales, up from just 12% in 2022.

## Key Drivers

### Apple Silicon
Apple's M-series chips cemented ARM's position by delivering exceptional performance with very low power consumption, making MacBook Pro dominant in the premium segment.

### Snapdragon X Elite
Qualcomm's chip entered Windows with force, backed by Microsoft's **Copilot+**, delivering performance rivaling Intel Core Ultra's best.

### MediaTek Processors
MediaTek Dimensity is seeing notable success in the mid-range laptop segment in Asian markets.

## Linux Implications

This shift is accelerating ARM support in the Linux community:
- **Debian/Ubuntu ARM64** has reached excellent maturity
- **Fedora Asahi** for Apple Silicon is in great shape
- Snapdragon X Elite Linux support is progressing quickly

## Outlook

Analysts expect ARM's share to surpass 50% by 2028.
